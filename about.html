Our Story

BNI AMIGOS is a premier business networking chapter based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh. We are part of Business Network International (BNI), the world's largest business networking and referral organization.

Our chapter consists of 85+ dedicated business professionals who meet weekly to share referrals, build relationships, and grow their businesses together.

Founded on the principle of "Givers Gain," we believe that by helping others succeed, we create opportunities for our own success.
